Which Country & Leagues Has Most Promising U21 Soccer Talents Today?

Across the top leagues, young soccer players under 21 are striving to make their mark. This narrative visualization helps identify which league and country performs best in discovering tommorrow stars. It is interesting that countries whose leagues have more U-21 players usually produce better-performing U-21 players, even if their leagues are not recognized as the most competitive in the world.

Data source: Kaggle - All Football Players Stats (2024-25)
